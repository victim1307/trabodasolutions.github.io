About Traboda

Traboda is an excellent platform for doing CTF challenges of various difficulty levels, as well as a perfect place for beginners to acquire more concepts and ideas about various CTF challenge categories. The site is easily navigable and user-friendly, and it assists us in identifying areas of interest.
Visit app.traboda.com to learn and master cyber security and hacking skills online through solving challenges hands-on.
There are different categories of Challenges such as :
1.Android
2.Cryptography
3.Forensic
4.Misc
5.Pwn
6.Reverse Engineering
7.Steganography
8.Web Exploitation


Participating in CTF activities and competing in groups helps you learn the meaning of purpose. In order to reach the goal, team members must work together. It also helps you to meet people who share your interests.

CTFs are the most successful way to learn and develop information security skills such as network manipulation, reverse engineering, binary exploitation, forensics, and more.

CTFs help you to refine your problem-solving and analytical skills for use in the workplace. CTF problems are typically focused on real-world security bugs and incidents
